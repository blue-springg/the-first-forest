The First Forest

A quiet story of love, memory, and healing.

After six years apart, Wen Yifan and Sang Yan unexpectedly meet again in a bar. Once classmates, their past is filled with unspoken feelings and painful misunderstandings—Wen Yifan had a crush on Sang Yan, but rejected his feelings, causing distance between them.

Fate brings them under the same roof, where they must confront their past and the fragility of the present. Wen Yifan’s sleepwalking — triggered by family trauma — leads her to Sang Yan’s room, intertwining their lives in unexpected ways.

This drama captures the delicate process of healing and rediscovering love, much like the forest it’s named after — full of quiet strength, mystery, and deep roots.

Watching Wen Yifan’s story made me long for a love that is gentle, patient, and profound — a love that feels like home.
